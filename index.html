What is Anusodhana ?

Anusodhana is a national level technical fest hosted by the College of Engineering Acharya Nagarjuna university . We here at anusodhana are intended to promote rich culture and heritage through the theme : "WE SALUTE THE SOLIDERS" this MARCH 2020.At Anusodhana we are packed up with a enormous list of technical and non technical events that make this two day event striking.
